City Rules

Rules keep the city clean.

Read these rules carefully before applying. They protect serious roleplay, fair scenes and the overall BASIC RP experience.

1. Respect & Community Behavior

Respect every player and staff member. BASIC RP should feel welcoming and serious for everyone.

  • No racism, sexism, hate speech, harassment, bullying or toxicity.
  • No personal insults, drama or disrespect in public chats.
  • Do not argue with staff during scenes. Use tickets for reports or appeals.
  • Threats, doxxing or real-life attacks are strictly forbidden.

2. Stay In Character

You must stay in character during RP scenes and keep the immersion alive.

  • Do not break RP by talking about admins, Discord, clips or real-life issues.
  • Do not stop a scene because you are unhappy with the outcome.
  • Finish the scene first, then report problems later through tickets.
  • Use realistic language, emotions and reactions for your character.

3. RDM & VDM

Random killing or vehicle attacks are not allowed.

  • Do not kill, shoot or attack someone without proper RP reason.
  • Do not run players over with vehicles unless the scene gives a realistic reason.
  • Every conflict needs build-up, story and valid motivation.
  • Revenge killing is not allowed.

4. Metagaming

Metagaming means using outside information inside the city.

  • Do not use Discord, livestreams, clips or OOC chats to gain in-game information.
  • Do not tell friends your location through Discord during active RP.
  • Do not recognize masked players without a valid RP reason.
  • Your character must learn information naturally inside the city.

5. Powergaming

Powergaming means forcing unrealistic actions or unfair outcomes.

  • Do not force another player into a situation without giving them a fair chance to respond.
  • Do not use unrealistic actions your character could not actually do.
  • Do not abuse game mechanics, animations, bugs or scripts for advantage.
  • Roleplay injuries, fear and consequences properly.

6. Value Of Life

Your character should care about survival and danger.

  • Fear weapons when they are aimed at you.
  • Do not act fearless during robberies, kidnappings or hostage situations.
  • Do not rush armed players without a realistic reason.
  • React naturally to crashes, injuries, threats and dangerous scenes.

7. New Life Rule

Death or serious injury must have consequences.

  • Do not return to the same scene immediately after death.
  • Do not seek revenge after respawning.
  • If your character is fully killed, do not remember exact scene details.
  • Follow EMS and staff instructions after major injuries.

8. Combat Logging

Leaving the server to avoid RP is forbidden.

  • Do not disconnect during police chases, arrests, fights, robberies or EMS scenes.
  • If your game crashes, reconnect as soon as possible.
  • If you cannot reconnect, contact staff and explain the situation.

9. Criminal RP

Criminal RP must be realistic, balanced and story-based.

  • Robberies need planning, RP and proper interaction.
  • Hostages must be treated fairly and included in the scene.
  • No constant violence, chain robberies or low-effort crime RP.
  • Gang conflicts must have valid reasons and story progression.

10. Police, EMS & Government RP

Emergency and government roles are part of the city story.

  • Do not bait police for no reason.
  • Do not attack EMS without strong RP reason.
  • Roleplay injuries properly with EMS.
  • Respect legal procedures and court/government RP when available.

11. Driving & Realism

Driving should match the situation and vehicle type.

  • No unrealistic stunt driving during normal RP.
  • No fail driving sports cars off-road unless realistic.
  • React properly to crashes and damage.
  • Respect traffic when not in emergency or criminal scenes.

12. Staff Decisions

Staff decisions keep the server organized and fair.

  • Do not argue in public channels.
  • Use tickets for reports, ban appeals, refunds or support.
  • Provide clips or proof when possible.
  • Lying to staff can lead to stronger punishment.
